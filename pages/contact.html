Frequently Asked Questions

What areas do you deliver to? +
We currently operate across central and south Phnom Penh including Khan Toul Kouk, Khan Chamcarmon, Khan Doun Penh, and Khan Prampi Makara. We're expanding to new areas monthly. You can check if your address is in our service zone using our app.
How long does delivery take? +
Standard delivery takes 25-30 minutes from order placement. Our AI optimizes routes to ensure the fastest possible delivery while maintaining the highest quality of our products. Express deliveries are available during peak hours.
How much does delivery cost? +
Delivery fees range from $1.50 to $3.00 depending on your location and order value. Subscription members enjoy free delivery on all orders. Orders above certain values also qualify for free delivery.
Are the drones safe? +
Absolutely! Our drones are equipped with advanced safety systems including collision avoidance, redundant sensors, and automatic return-to-base capabilities. They undergo rigorous testing and comply with all local regulations. Your safety is our top priority.
What payment methods do you accept? +
We accept all major credit cards, debit cards, mobile wallets (ABA, ACLEDA), and bank transfers. All payments are secured with encryption technology to protect your financial information.
Can I track my delivery? +
Yes! Our mobile app provides real-time GPS tracking of your drone delivery. You can see your drone's exact location, estimated arrival time, and even receive notifications as it approaches your address.
What if there's an issue with my order? +
Customer satisfaction is our priority. If there's any issue with your order, contact our support team immediately via phone, email, or the app. We offer replacements or refunds for unsatisfactory orders within 24 hours.
Do you offer corporate/bulk orders? +
Yes! We provide special B2B services for offices, hotels, restaurants, and events. Contact our corporate team for customized quotes, bulk pricing, and dedicated account management.
