About Aigrit

Revolutionizing Bread Delivery with AI and Autonomous Technology

Our Mission & Vision

Mission

To deliver fresh, premium quality bread to every household in Phnom Penh using innovative AI autonomous drone technology. We believe in making quality bakery products accessible, affordable, and delivered with uncompromising freshness.

Vision

To become Southeast Asia's leading AI-powered autonomous delivery platform for artisan bread and bakery products, setting new standards for sustainability, speed, and customer experience.
