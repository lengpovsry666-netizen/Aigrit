## 🛠️ Customization Quick Tips

### Update Company Info
Search and replace in all files:
- "AiGrit" → Your company name
- "+32 (0)2 1234 5678" → Your phone
- "info@aigrit.be" → Your email
- "Brussels, Belgium" → Your location

### Change Color Scheme
Edit top of `assets/css/styles.css`:
```css
--primary-color: #2563eb;      /* Change to your brand color */
--accent-color: #f59e0b;       /* Change to your accent color */
```

### Modify Services
Edit `services.html` to add/remove:
- Service descriptions
- Pricing tiers
- Feature lists
- Tier names

### Update Team
Edit `about.html`:
- Team member names
- Team roles
- Team descriptions
- Add/remove team members
